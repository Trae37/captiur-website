How Captiur Works

Three steps to reclaim your focus.

Captiur captures your entire development workspace and restores it instantly. No more rebuilding context after every interruption.

1

Capture

One click or hotkey snapshots your entire workspace: open tabs, IDE windows, terminal sessions, and your mental notes about what you were doing.

  • VS Code / Cursor workspaces
  • Browser tabs (Chrome, Brave, Edge)
  • Terminal processes
2

Find

Browse your workspace timeline or search by project, file path, or notes. Every snapshot is indexed and searchable.

  • Visual timeline browser
  • Full-text search
  • Filter by project or date
3

Restore

Click restore and watch your entire workspace come back to life. Apps open, tabs load, terminals reconnect - exactly as you left them.

  • One-click restore
  • Partial restore support
  • Context notes displayed
