Meet Captiur

Captiur is a desktop application that captures and restores your entire development workspace. It saves your tabs, IDEs, terminals, and notes so you can pick up exactly where you left off.

Privacy First

All your workspace data is stored locally on your machine. Nothing is sent to the cloud unless you explicitly choose to sync.

Core Pillars

Captiur is built around three core concepts that work together to eliminate workspace reconstruction time:

Workspace Capture

Snapshot your entire workspace including open apps, tabs, and files in one click.

Instant Restore

Resume exactly where you left off. Restore apps, tabs, and windows in seconds.

Session Notes

Add context to captures so you remember what you were thinking and what's next.

Who is Captiur for?

Captiur is designed for developers who frequently context-switch between projects or get interrupted during deep work. If you've ever:

  • Lost 20+ minutes reopening tabs, terminals, and IDE windows after a meeting
  • Forgotten what you were doing after being interrupted by Slack or email
  • Struggled to get back into flow after switching between multiple projects
  • Wished you could just "unpause" your work exactly where you left it

Pro Tip

Enable auto-save to automatically capture your workspace at regular intervals. You'll never lose more than a few minutes of context.
